Rovers have been in a loveless relationship with Valentine’s Day for almost 50 years

It’s now no league wins on February 14 since 1976.
Author

Pete Brown

Published

February 21, 2023

Bradford

Last Tuesday’s 2-0 reverse at Valley Parade

This was Rovers’ sixteenth Valentine’s Day fixture, the first being a home game with Barrow in 1925.

At first glance, the club’s record doesn’t look bad.
